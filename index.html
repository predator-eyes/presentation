Predator Eyes Ground Station

Advanced communication and control system for drone operations

Purpose

The ground station is designed to:

  • Transmit communication between the drone operator and repeater
  • Ensure the safety of the drone operator during piloting
  • Provide a robust and reliable control system for drone operations

Components

The ground control system consists of:

  • Mast and antenna system
  • Control station
  • Repeater
  • Custom tranciever PCB pair between station and repeater designed and manufactured in UA

Mast and Antenna System

Mast Specifications:

  • 9-meter aluminum mast
  • Reinforced clamps with easy-to-use fixing elements
  • Adjustable support legs for various terrains
  • Pegs for fixation
  • Foldable for easy transportation

Antenna System:

  • 1.2/4.9/5.8 GHz receiving antennas helical or planar type
  • 433-1020 MHz transmitting antenna

Control Station

Components:

  • Shock-resistant plastic transport case
  • up to 100m high-quality cable reel
  • 14-inch LED monitor - superslim, energy-efficient, adapted for military needs
  • Video recording device with 16 GB storage
  • Battery charge level indicator
  • 6S2P battery
  • Type-C output for charging mobile devices
  • XT-60 output for powering additional devices

Repeater

Waterproof remote repeater features:

  • 1.2/4.9/5.8 GHz receiver
  • 2W transmitter - base frequencies 433/730-830/915-950 MHz, custom control frequencies available
  • Active cooling system
  • Convenient quick-release mechanism

Quality Assurance

Rigorous Testing and Certification:

  • Each ground station is thoroughly tested on our own drones before shipment
  • Successfully passed codification with the Ukrainian Ministry of Defense
  • Integrated into multiple FPV complexes
  • Extensively field-tested under real operational conditions

As manufacturers, we can modify the station's configuration to meet your specific needs.
